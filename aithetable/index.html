🪑 What is The AI Table?

The AI Table is a learning platform built by and for Gen X women who are curious about AI and automation. Think of it as your kitchen table — a safe, warm spot where smart women share what they're learning, ask questions without judgment, and figure out what's next together. Right now you can grab the ebook. The community and live office hours are coming soon — we're building them out now and they'll be worth the wait.

📖 What's in the ebook?

AI For The Rest Of Us is an 8-chapter guide with 200 copy-paste AI prompts. It covers AI basics, using AI at work, life management, building websites with Claude Code, web hosting, SEO, vibe coding, and workflow automation with connectors like Gmail, Calendar, Slack, and Notion. It's $27 and an instant download.

🌱 Do I need to know anything about AI to start?

Not at all. Whether you've been tinkering with ChatGPT for months or you're not quite sure what a "prompt" is — you belong here. We meet you where you are. Some members are complete beginners; others are building automations. All are welcome at this table.

🛠️ What AI tools do you cover?

We're tool-agnostic. The ebook and community cover ChatGPT, Claude, Zapier, Make.com, Midjourney, Canva AI, Notion AI, and more — from prompt engineering to full workflow automation. If there's a tool you're curious about, bring it to the table.

📹 What are office hours?

Office hours are live Zoom sessions where you can bring your AI and automation questions, share what you're working on, or just listen and learn. We do tool spotlight deep dives, open Q&A sessions, and breaking news calls when something big drops in AI. Think group coaching, but way more fun.

👩‍💻 Is this only for women?

The ebook is for everyone — men included. If you want to learn AI in plain language with 200 ready-to-use prompts, it's for you. The community, however, is intentionally women-centered. We know how tricky online communities can be for women, so we built a space where they can share, ask questions, and learn without the noise. That's the focus — and it's why it works.

👋 Who is Blagica Bottigliero?

Blagica is an Emmy Award-winning digital strategist with 25+ years in digital. She's worked at Edelman Digital, Motorola Mobility, and Orbitz. She co-founded Bottles Nation (zero to $1M in 10 months, bootstrapped). Today she runs Zlato Labs and created The AI Table to help Gen X women master AI on their own terms.
