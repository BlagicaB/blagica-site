About

A Digital OG.
Still building.

I'm a first-gen Macedonian kid from Metro Detroit who ended up in the front row of the internet's entire history. From CD-ROMs at Giant Step to joining Orbitz early in their eMarketing team — through the dot-com crash, through LinkedIn arguments, through Friendster and MySpace and TikTok — I've watched everything and helped build a lot of it.

I've run global social at Motorola Mobility. Led digital strategy as VP at Edelman. Co-founded Bottles Nation, one of the first virtual tasting platforms in the US, and hit $1M in 10 months. Built and ran the full marketing operation for a DTC THC beverage brand in a fully regulated category — standing up GA4, cAPI, attribution, paid social, PR, experiential, connected TV, and out-of-home from scratch.

What I do best is translate ambition into traction. Crisp messaging. Plans that ladder to revenue. Creative testing loops you can run in hours. Analytics that executives and operators both actually understand.

I'm also a writer. A builder. And back home in Metro Detroit, where my family first put down roots near 94 and Chene.
